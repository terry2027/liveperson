How to Buy Load with DiskarTech

  1. From the DiskarTech dashboard, tap Buy Load.
  2. Enter the mobile number you want to load up.
  3. Choose the load denomination or promo you want to purchase and tap Confirm.
  4. An OTP will be generated and sent to your number. Enter the OTP to verify the transaction.
