DiskarTech is certainly earning its “super app” moniker, thanks to the useful features it has. From paying bills to buying insurance, here’s how you can maximize your DiskarTech app.

How to Buy Insurance with DiskarTech

  1. From the DiskarTech dashboard, tap “Insurance.”
  2. Choose the insurance product from the following list:
    • PanProtect COVID-19 Insurance
    • Sari-Sari Store Insurance
    • Dengue Cash 
    • Commuters Personal Accident
    • Ridesafe Personal Accident
    • Super Personal Accident
  3. After choosing, tap Avail na! and provide your occupation and beneficiary.
  4. Confirm your details and tap Confirm.
  5. An OTP will be generated and sent to your number. Enter the OTP to verify the transaction.
  6. Your Certificate of Coverage (COC) will be sent to you via email within three to five business days.
  7. To file a claim, visit the Malayan Insurance claims website.[3]
